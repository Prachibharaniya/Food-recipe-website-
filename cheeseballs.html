Crispy Cheese Balls Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup grated cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • 1/2 cup mashed potatoes
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander (optional)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix grated cheese, mashed potatoes, flour, salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  2. Shape the mixture into small balls.
  3. Coat each ball with breadcrumbs.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and deep-fry the cheese balls until golden brown.
  5. Remove and place on paper towels to drain excess oil.
  6. Serve hot with ketchup or your favorite dip.

Enjoy your crispy cheese balls!

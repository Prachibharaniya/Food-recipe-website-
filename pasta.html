Pasta Alfredo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, melt butter over medium heat and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer.
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese and mix until smooth.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat.
  7. Garnish with parsley and serve warm.

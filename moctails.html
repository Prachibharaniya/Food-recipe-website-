Refreshing Citrus Mint Mocktail

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup fresh orange juice
  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey or simple syrup
  • 1/2 cup sparkling water
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Ice cubes
  • Orange and lime slices for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a glass, muddle a few mint leaves with honey or simple syrup.
  2. Add fresh orange and lime juice.
  3. Fill the glass with ice cubes.
  4. Top with sparkling water and stir gently.
  5. Garnish with orange and lime slices.
  6. Enjoy your refreshing mocktail!

Spicy Schezwan Noodles

Ingredients:

  • 200g noodles
  • 2 tbsp Schezwan sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped vegetables (carrot, bell peppers, cabbage)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Boil noodles as per package instructions, drain, and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add chopped vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add Schezwan sauce, soy sauce, vinegar, chili flakes, and black pepper. Mix well.
  5. Add boiled noodles, toss everything together, and cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.

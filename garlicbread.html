Garlic Bread Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 loaf of French bread
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Slice the French bread in half lengthwise.
  3. In a small bowl, mix melted butter, minced garlic, parsley, and salt.
  4. Brush the garlic butter mixture evenly over the bread.
  5. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese if using.
  6. Place on a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden and crispy.
  7. Slice and serve warm. Enjoy!

Cheese Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) instant yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, yeast, and salt. Gradually add warm water and olive oil.
  3. Knead the dough for about 5 minutes until smooth, then let it rest for 10 minutes.
  4. Roll out the dough into a round shape and place it on a pizza tray.
  5. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  6. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top and add dried oregano.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Remove from the oven, slice, and enjoy!

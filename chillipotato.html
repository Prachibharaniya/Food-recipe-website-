Honey Chilli Potato Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3 large potatoes (cut into thin fries)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp red chilli flakes
  • 1/2 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  • 1/2 cup sliced onions and bell peppers

Instructions:

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into thin fries and soak them in water for 10 minutes.
  2. Drain and coat them with cornflour, all-purpose flour, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Deep fry until crispy and golden brown. Set aside.
  4. In a pan, heat 1 tbsp oil and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  5. Add sliced onions and bell peppers and stir-fry for a minute.
  6. Add soy sauce, ketchup, red chilli flakes, vinegar, and honey. Mix well.
  7. Toss in the fried potatoes and coat them with the sauce evenly.
  8. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top and serve hot.

Enjoy your delicious Honey Chilli Potatoes!
